Maze Game Instructions

Welcome to the Maze Game! Your goal is simple:

  • Use your mouse to navigate from the Start (S) to the Finish (E).
  • Avoid touching the walls as you go! If you touch a wall, it's game over.
  • Try to complete the maze as fast as possible to get the best time!

Good luck, and have fun!
